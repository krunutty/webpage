---------Made In Abyss-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A Japanese manga series written by Akihito Tsukushi.

Has been serialized online since October 2012.

The story follows a girl named Riko who finds and befriends a robot named Reg

and descend with him into the titular"Abyss" in hope of finding her mother while exploring the Abyss hole.
